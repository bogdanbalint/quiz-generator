What you can do
Main capabilities available in the application.
Create an account and sign in
Users can register, log in securely and access their personal quiz workspace.
Generate quizzes with AI
OpenAI is used to create multiple-choice quizzes based on a chosen topic, difficulty and optional source text.
Solve quizzes and review results
Each quiz can be completed online, scored automatically and reviewed together with explanations.
Receive email notifications
SendGrid is used to deliver a welcome email after registration and a quiz result email after submission.
How it works
Simple user flow from start to finish.
1. Register or sign in
Access the platform through a personal account.
2. Generate a quiz
Choose a topic, select a difficulty and let the AI prepare the questions.
3. Solve and submit
Answer the questions online and receive an instant score.
4. Review and revisit
Check explanations, browse quiz history and receive results by email.
Cloud services used
The application integrates external cloud services for its core functionality.
OpenAI
Used for AI-powered quiz generation, including questions, answer options and explanations.
SendGrid
Used for transactional emails such as the welcome email and the quiz result summary.
Cloud deployment
The application will be published online together with its database so it can be demonstrated remotely.
